How To Get a Personal Loan in 5 Easy Steps

Step 1: Determine Your Requirement

Figure out why you need a Personal Loan and how much you need. For example, you may need a loan to fund your wedding or to renovate your home. Determine the amount you need, be it Rs. 1 lac or Rs. 10 lac.

Step 2: Check Loan Eligibility

Once you know how much you need, check your eligibility. Use online eligibility calculators like the HDFC Bank Personal Loan Eligibility Calculator to determine how much you can borrow. HDFC Bank offers loans Rs. 40 lac.

Step 3: Calculate Monthly Instalments

Use an online EMI tool to estimate your monthly repayments. Adjust the interest rate and tenure to fit your monthly income, such as on the HDFC Bank Personal Loan EMI calculator. HDFC Bank offers EMIs starting at Rs. 1878 per lac.

Step 4: Approach the Bank

Apply for a Personal Loan with HDFC Bank through various methods: via NetBanking, online on their website, at an ATM, or by visiting a branch.
